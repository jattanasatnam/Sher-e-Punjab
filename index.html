Care and Preservation Guide

Washing Instructions

  • Always wash in *cold water* with mild detergent.
  • Hand wash is highly recommended for silk and rubia fabrics.
  • Do *not* use bleach or harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid direct sunlight when drying to prevent color fading.

Folding for Storage

  1. Shake out the turban fully to remove dust and creases.
  2. Fold the cloth lengthwise into a manageable band.
  3. Roll the band tightly from one end to the other.
  4. Store in a dry, cool area, preferably wrapped in soft muslin cloth.
